How to add a new module release in the central maven repository ?

Example for met4j-core.
- Commit everything, merge into master and push
- Add these lines in the pom.xml file:

<scm>
    <developerConnection>scm:git:https://forgemia.inra.fr/metexplore/met4j/blob/master/met4j-core/</developerConnection>
</scm>
...

 <build>
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
        <artifactId>maven-release-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>3.0.0-M1</version>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
    ....
  </build>
